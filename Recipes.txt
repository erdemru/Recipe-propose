Brownie: dark chocolate, sugar, flour qty 100gr, 10gr, 200gr, cal 466, dessert
Mix together the flour, water, sugar and dark chocolate that you melt before, make a batter.
Then, pour the batter into an baking pan.
Transfer the pan to a 325-degree oven and bake for 40 to 45 minutes.
Allow the brownies to cool completely before slicing and serving.
Enjoy!

Cake: flour, egg qty 300gr, 3gr, cal 251, dessert
Mix together the flour and egg.
Pour the batter into a cake mold when it's be homogeneous.
Transfer the mold to a 325-degree oven and bake for 30-35 minutes.
Allow the cake to cool completely before slicing and serving.
Enjoy!

Cheesecake: cream cheese, lemon sauce qty 400gr, 4gr, cal 321, dessert
